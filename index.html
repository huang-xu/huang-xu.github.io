Huang Xu


I am a PhD student in Northwestern Polytechnical University, and my advisor is Professor Zhiwen Yu. I am now supported by China Scholarship Council as a Visiting Scholar at Business School, Rutgers-the State University of New Jersey under advisory of Professor Hui Xiong from 2015.
